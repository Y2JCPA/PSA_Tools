US-Israel Treaty — Key Provisions

Article 11 — Dividends: 25% general rate; 12.5% if beneficial owner is a company holding ≥10% of voting stock.

Article 12 — Interest: 17.5% general rate; 0% for interest paid to the government or central bank.

Article 13 — Royalties: 15% general rate; 10% for industrial royalties (patents, know-how, etc.).

Article 17 — Pensions: Generally taxable only in the country of residence, subject to conditions.

Article 24 — Teachers & Researchers: Exempt from host-country tax for up to 2 years.

Article 24 — Students & Trainees: Up to $3,000/year exempt for living expenses.
